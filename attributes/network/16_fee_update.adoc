= Fee Update Attribute (#16)
:cddl: ./cddl/

The fee update attribute (#16) allows neighborhoods to update their internal fee tables.

Neighborhoods MAY limit access to this attribute to only the owner of the neighborhood.

Neighborhood who exposes this attribute MUST also expose the fee attribute (#15).
